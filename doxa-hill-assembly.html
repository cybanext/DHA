About Us

A church where every life finds its calling

Doxa Hill Assembly is a Spirit-filled community of believers in Accra, Ghana — founded on the deep conviction that every person carries a God-given purpose and a divine destiny waiting to be unlocked.

Our name — Doxa, meaning glory — defines everything we do. From worship to outreach, from counselling to community, we exist to bring glory to God in all things, and to raise Kingdom ambassadors who carry that same mandate into their families, workplaces, and nations.

Since 2018, we have been a family marked by authenticity, love, and a relentless hunger for God's presence. Whether you are discovering faith for the first time or deepening a walk of many years — you belong here.

Mission
Make disciples who make disciples

Statement of Faith

  • We believe in the Holy Trinity — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit
  • The Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God
  • Salvation comes by grace alone, through faith in Jesus Christ
  • We believe in water baptism and the baptism of the Holy Spirit
  • The gifts of the Spirit are active and available for believers today
  • We believe in the bodily resurrection and the Second Coming of Christ

Leadership

Meet our pastors

EK
Rev. Emmanuel Kweku
Senior Pastor & Founder

A passionate preacher with over 15 years in ministry. Rev. Kweku carries a burning heart for souls and a vision to raise godly leaders across Africa and the nations. He is known for his depth in the Word, genuine accessibility, and extraordinary love for people.

AK
Pastor Abena Kweku
Co-Pastor · Women's Ministry

A woman of grace and uncommon strength. Pastor Abena leads with compassion and precision, championing women's empowerment and family discipleship. She believes every woman holds a royal identity waiting to be fully unveiled.

KM
Pastor Kofi Mensah
Associate Pastor · Youth Lead

Dynamic, relatable, and Spirit-led — Pastor Kofi is the heartbeat of Doxa Hill's youth movement. He equips the next generation to live boldly, think critically, and serve selflessly in a world that desperately needs Christ.

Ministries

Find where you fit

Every ministry is a family. Find where you belong, grow, and make a difference that outlasts a lifetime.

01
Children's Ministry

A safe, joyful, Spirit-filled space where children encounter Jesus through age-appropriate worship, Bible stories, and creative activities.

02
Youth Ministry

Equipping teenagers and young adults to navigate life with wisdom and boldness. The youth are not the church of tomorrow — they are the church of today.

03
Women's Ministry

Celebrating and empowering women in every season. Through fellowship, mentorship, and the Word, every woman discovers her royal identity in Christ.

04
Men's Ministry

Building strong men of God — husbands, fathers, and leaders who walk in integrity, accountability, and genuine Kingdom purpose. Iron sharpens iron.

05
Worship & Choir

Leading the congregation into the presence of God through music and praise. Our team is passionate, diverse, and deeply committed to anointed worship.

06
Outreach & Evangelism

Taking the love of Christ into schools, communities, hospitals, and nations. We are compelled by love to go and tell the world of His goodness.
